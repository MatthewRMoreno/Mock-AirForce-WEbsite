About CB
The Business Information Systems and Requirements Directorate administers civil engineer information technology (IT) through data management, IT governance and system configuration. This directorate is responsible for safeguarding and sustaining our civil engineer IT solutions to ensure the Air Force Civil Engineer Center is equipped to deliver the functional​ capabilities required for mission success. This directorate, made up of three divisions, also provides command action support by managing AFCEC's workflow processes and corporate infrastructure, overseeing and implementing strategic programs, and delivering missioncritical functions to Tyndall Air Force Base, Florida. Other primary mission capabilities of the directorate include human resources oversight, unit training and unit deployment management, and recognition and civilian development programs for the enterprise.
