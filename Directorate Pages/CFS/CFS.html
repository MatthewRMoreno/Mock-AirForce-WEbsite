About CFS
The Strategic Design and Construction Execution Division delivers world-class design and construction management for military construction in the CENTCOM and AFRICOM areas of responsibilities along with benchmark centralized design and construction execution, as lead execution agent on behalf of the both CONUS and remote OCUNUS installations, for facility sustainment, restoration, and modernization initiatives to include the Defense Logistics Agency, the National Air and Space Agency and the National Reconnaissance Office.
