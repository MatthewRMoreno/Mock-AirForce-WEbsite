About CFM
The AFCEC Military Design and Construction Division provides design and contruction of Built Infrastructure which provides the power projection platforms of Air and Space Operatoins. We deliver safe, quality design and construction results, within effectively communicated time and cost parameters, through a diverse staff of military, civilian and contracted team members. The Division also provides programmatic guidance and expertise for Air Force Military Construction (MILCON) programs.
